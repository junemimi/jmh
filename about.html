About Me

Hi! I’m June – a software engineer 👩🏻‍💻, creative problem solver 💭, and lifelong learner 🧠. With a BS in Informatics from the University of Washington, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for the entire tech process, from user research and planning to design, development, and testing. While I specialize in Swift and iOS/macOS development, I’m always excited to be involved in all aspects of a project, especially when it comes to design!

I love leveraging my skills to create meaningful and impactful tech solutions that solve real problems and improve lives. My work experience spans software development and data science, and I’m always looking for new ways to grow, learn, and give back to the community.

My friends describe me as enthusiastic, observant, ambitious, and bold. I thrive on discovering new things, stepping outside my comfort zone, and embracing new challenges and adventures!

Outside of work, I’m love baking 🍰, photography 📸, traveling ✈️, staying active 💪, and listening to music 🎶. During my free time, you can probably find me hanging out with friends or filming a baking video.
