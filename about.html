About Me

Hi! I'm June and I'm a software engineer 👩🏻‍💻, creative thinker 💭, and a constant learner 🧠. I love coding in Swift and developing iOS/macOS applications, and web development. My degree from the University in Washington in Informatics (BS) taught me to love and pay equal attention to the entire development process - from user research, project planning, and design to development and testing. Although I specialize in coding, I love any opportunity to take charge and be involved in that full process (especially design)!

I use my passions for always learning, solving problems, and helping others to create compassionate and meaningful tech solutions. I primarily have experience in software development and data science, and am always looking for opportunities to broaden my skillset, help others, and give back to the community.

My friends describe me as enthusiastic, observant, ambitious, and bold. I love any opportunity to discover something new, become a better version of myself, step outside of my comfort zone, and go on exciting advantures!

Outside of work, I love photography 📸, baking 🍰, traveling ✈️, exercise 💪, and music 🎶. In my free time, you will probably find me at the beach with my friends or filming a baking video.

Thanks for checking out my website and I hope you enjoy! 🌺
